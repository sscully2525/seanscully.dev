# Startup Ideas for Sean

## Criteria
- Consumer-facing (TikTok-market-able)
- Finance/stock angle where possible
- Solo-founder friendly
- AI/ML leverage
- NYC advantage

---

## 1. **PaperTrade AI** 🎯 *Top Pick*
**What:** AI-powered paper trading simulator with TikTok-style short-form trade explanations

**The Hook:**
- Users paper trade with fake money
- AI generates 15-60 second "why this trade worked/failed" videos
- TikTok viral potential: "I turned $10k into $50k in 30 days (simulated)"
- Leaderboards, streaks, social proof

**Tech:**
- Real-time market data (Polygon.io, Alpaca - free tiers)
- LLM generates trade narratives
- Video generation (HeyGen, D-ID, or simple text-to-speech + charts)
- LangGraph for multi-step trade analysis workflow

**Monetization:**
- Freemium: free paper trading, $10/mo for AI insights
- Affiliate: real broker referrals when users go live
- B2B: white-label for financial educators

**Why it works:**
- Trading content is huge on TikTok/YouTube
- No regulatory risk (simulated only)
- Clear viral loop (share your wins)

## 2. **ReceiptRadar**
**What:** AI that reads your receipts and finds you money

**The Hook:**
- Snap receipt → AI extracts items
- Auto-matches against: price protection policies, cashback apps, warranties, return windows
- "You overpaid $12 on these shoes. Target will price-match until Friday."
- TikTok: "This app found me $847 last year"

**Tech:**
- OCR (Google Vision, AWS Textract)
- LLM for policy parsing and matching
- Browser automation for claims
- LangGraph for multi-step recovery workflows

**Monetization:**
- Freemium: 5 receipts/mo free, unlimited $5/mo
- Success fee: 10% of recovered money
- B2B: API for cashback apps

## 3. **SubStacked**
**What:** AI newsletter operator for busy professionals

**The Hook:**
- You pick a niche (AI, finance, climate)
- AI researches, writes, and sends weekly newsletters
- You just review and hit publish
- TikTok: "I started a newsletter with 0 writing. 10k subs in 3 months."

**Tech:**
- Web scraping + RSS aggregation
- LLM for curation and writing
- LangGraph for multi-source synthesis
- Automated scheduling (Beehiiv/Substack APIs)

**Monetization:**
- SaaS: $29/mo per newsletter
- Revenue share on premium subscriptions
- Done-for-you: $500/mo fully managed

## 4. **MeetingMoney**
**What:** Calculate the real cost of every meeting

**The Hook:**
- Calendar integration
- Pulls salary data (levels.fyi, self-reported)
- Shows meeting cost in real-time: "This 30-min standup costs $340"
- Suggests async alternatives
- TikTok: "My company spent $50k on meetings today"

**Tech:**
- Google/Outlook Calendar APIs
- LLM for meeting purpose classification
- Simple cost calculator
- Slack/Teams bot for real-time alerts

**Monetization:**
- B2B SaaS: $5/user/mo
- Enterprise: custom pricing
